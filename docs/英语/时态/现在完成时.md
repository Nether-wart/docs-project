# 现在完成时
## 定义
过去发生的动作 **持续到现在**/**对现在产生影响**
## 结构
hava/has (助动词) + 过去分词
## 时间状语
1. already	//肯定
2. yet	//否定或疑问 ,放句尾
---
3. ever	//肯定
4. never	//否定
---
5. just
6. before
7. recently
---
8. once,twice,three times (次数)
---
9. over the years 在这些年里
10. in/during the past [num] years 在过去的[num]年里
11. these days 如今
---
12. 
	-  so far 到目前为止
	-  up to now 
	-  till/until now 
---
13. 
	- since  + [过去时间] +
		- 过去时间  //例:since then 
		- 句子(一般过去时)
	- for  + [过去时间] //例:for years 数年
		- 转换:  since 2000  ==  for 25 years 

## 转换
1. borrowed == hava/has kept
2. bought == hava/has had
3. began/started == hava/has been on
4. finished/stopped/ended == hava/has been over
5. came/went/arrive == hava/has been in + [大型地点] / at + [小型地点]
6. left == hava/has been away (from)
7. joined == hava/has been in / a member of
